A Study in Contradictions

From Humble Beginnings

Born and raised among the salt marshes of Southeast Texas, my roots are firmly planted in a blue-collar, union-driven world that taught me the value of a hard day's work and the grit required to change one’s circumstances.

A Turning Point

Leaving the world of high-pressure marketing for technical writing was more than a career move; it was a moral recalibration. By shifting into medical device documentation, I found a way to align my professional precision with work that truly serves the public good.

The Next Chapter

I am currently architecting a book review platform designed to offer nuance over noise. My goal is to build a community that values deep interest categories and diverse perspectives, ensuring every reader finds their 'one true sentence.'
